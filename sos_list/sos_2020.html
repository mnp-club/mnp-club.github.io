Summer of Science 2020
Summer of Science is an initiative by the Maths and Physics Club to encourage and help discover the joy of learning.

Summer of Science Projects 2020

High Energy Astrophysics
Dive into High Energy Astrophysics with Yatin Jindal and explore the most extreme bodies of the universe in the most extreme circumstances and conditions.
Graph Theory and Graph Algorithms
Aakriti's aim in this project is to explore this realm of Discrete Mathematics and study the theoretical aspects of Graph Theory as well as the various algorithms modeled on graphs and their application in varied fields of study.
Basics of Coding Theory
Ever wondered how digitalised data is transmitted? How the aberrations in recieved data resolved? Abhinav Gupta attempts to understand mathematics behind varoius encoding and decoding methodologies that led to evolution of Coding Theory.
Financial Mathematics
Dishank Jindal attempts to explore the working of financial markets and the mathematical reasoning behind the components the market comprises of.
Space Flight Mechanics
This project by Abhinav Biswas is an attempt to know how probes are sent to different celestial objects along with the mathematics and physics behind it,which may interest even non-specialists in the subject concerned with space-flight mechanics.
