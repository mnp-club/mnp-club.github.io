Summer of Science Projects 2023

Fluid Mechanics
Take a look at the report by Archit Mundada on fluid mechanics, the branch of physics concerned with the mechanics of fluids (liquids, gases, and plasmas) and the forces on them
Abstract Algebra
The roots of continuous mathematical symmetry lie within the realm of group theory or, more generally, Abstract Algebra. Here is a report by Nilabha Saha delving into Abstract Algebra.
Operating Systems
Take a look at Aman Sharma exploring operating system, the layer of software that manages a computer's resources for its users and their applications.
Algorithmic Trading
Algorithmic trading, also known as automated trading, is a fascinating field that combines computer programming and financial markets to execute successful trading. This report by Harshit Agarwal explores the same.
Criminology
The various notions and myths revolving around the work of a forensic psychologist, his authenticity, his use and then how prison systems affect various societies in different ways(penology) is what Vaishnavi Arun Kukde has tried to cover in her Report on criminology.
