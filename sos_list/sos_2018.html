Summer of Science Projects 2018

Quantum Computation
Whats more exciting than Quantum mechanics or Computer science? Both of them put together! The principles of Quantum mechanics allows computations which are exponentially faster than those possible classically. Deepak Gopalan studies the basics of Quantum Computation!
Probability and Statistics
Uncertainty.Randomness.Trends.Deviation- This report by Manjeet Singh briefly describes the concepts to study and exploit these terms. It motivates to apply probability theory to better understand everyday statistical situations.
Data Structures and Algorithms
A data structure is an arrangement of data in a computer's memory or even disk storage. Algorithms, on the other hand, are used to manipulate the data contained in these data structures as in searching and sorting. Meghana Verma worked on their implementation.
Statistics in Finance
With this report, Mrinal Agarwal tries to enter the world of the basic statistical terms used in finance. Have a look on it if you wish to introduce yourself to the basic idea of statistics in finance.
Automotive Engineering
Many of us get fascinated by the speed ,power and design of cars .Is their design just for show or some reason lies behind it? What's inside a car that speeds it upto 400kph ? Ayush Agrawal attempts to find the answers to all these questions.
